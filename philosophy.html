Investment philosophy

Ownership before activity.

Our approach is built around patience, selectivity, and discipline.

Business Quality First

Options are only one part of the process. The underlying business matters more. We are not interested in selling options on companies we would not want to own.

Valuation Discipline

A great company can still be a poor investment if purchased at the wrong price. The premium received is not free money. It is compensation for accepting risk.

Risk Management

Risk is not only volatility. It includes permanent capital loss, poor business quality, excessive leverage, emotional decision-making, and being forced to sell at the wrong time.

Long-Term Partnership

Our ideal partner thinks in years, not weeks. We are building for investors who value discipline over activity and ownership over speculation.
