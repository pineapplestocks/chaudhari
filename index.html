We seek to partner with a select group of families, founders, business owners, and professionals who share a long-term orientation and understand that disciplined investing requires patience, judgment, and emotional control.

Strategy

Collateralized Ownership Strategy.

We use options selectively, not as speculation, but as a disciplined way to pursue ownership at attractive prices. The first question is always whether the business is one we would be willing to own through a full cycle.

When quality, valuation, and downside protection line up, we may sell cash-secured puts to be paid for that patience. Assignment is not treated as a mistake; it is a planned path to ownership at a net entry price we believe is attractive.

Quality First

We focus on businesses with durable advantages, strong balance sheets, capable management, and long-term relevance.

Conservative Collateral

Cash collateral may be held in short-duration Treasury bills, Treasury money market funds, or similar cash-equivalent instruments where appropriate.

Clear Communication

Partners should understand what we are underwriting, where premium is being generated, and how we are thinking about risk.

We are comfortable being patient. We are comfortable holding cash. Most importantly, we are comfortable saying no.
