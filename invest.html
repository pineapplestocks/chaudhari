Family fund

How the family fund relationship works.

The process begins with a conversation about objectives, time horizon, liquidity needs, risk tolerance, and whether the fund's approach is appropriate. We would rather be selective than accept capital that does not fit the strategy.

The current minimum investment is $100,000, and partners should be prepared for a minimum hold period of one calendar year. This helps keep the capital base aligned with the fund's patient, collateralized approach.

If there is mutual interest, qualified prospective partners may receive additional materials and definitive documents. Any investment would be made only through those documents and only by investors who meet applicable eligibility standards.

What makes a good partner.

The right partner understands that disciplined investing can involve periods of waiting, holding cash, and passing on ideas that do not meet the standard. We look for people who prefer a clear process over constant activity.

We are best suited for investors who think in years, appreciate downside underwriting, and want direct communication about both opportunity and risk.
