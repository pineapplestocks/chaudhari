About

Founder-led and intentionally focused.

Chaudhari Partners was created for long-term investors who want a thoughtful public-market partner.

A private partnership for patient capital.

We are not trying to build a large asset-gathering platform. We are building a selective investment partnership with people who share our temperament, time horizon, and view of risk.

Our standard is simple: treat partner capital with care, avoid unnecessary complexity, communicate clearly, and remain disciplined when markets become emotional.

We seek partners who value:

  • Long-term thinking
  • Discipline over activity
  • Clear communication
  • Alignment over hype
  • A realistic view of risk
