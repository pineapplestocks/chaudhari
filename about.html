About

Founder-led and intentionally focused.

Chaudhari Partners Family Fund was created as a founder-led family fund for long-term investors who want a thoughtful public-market partner with a clear process and a calm view of risk.

A family fund for patient capital.

We are not trying to build a large asset-gathering platform. We are building a selective family fund with people who share our temperament, time horizon, and view of risk.

Our standard is simple: treat partner capital with care, avoid unnecessary complexity, communicate clearly, and remain disciplined when markets become emotional.

The family fund is designed around a small number of durable principles: understand the business first, underwrite the downside before pursuing return, and only act when the risk and reward are attractive enough to justify the commitment of capital.

How we work

Research-led, selective, and deliberately patient.

Our work begins with business quality. We look for companies with durable advantages, sensible balance sheets, capable leadership, and products or services that can remain relevant over time. We prefer to miss an opportunity rather than reach for activity.

When the price, business quality, and risk profile line up, we may use cash-secured put options as a disciplined way to generate premium or potentially acquire shares at an effective entry price we find attractive. The options process is an extension of ownership thinking, not a substitute for it.

Cash is treated as a position, not a failure to act. Waiting for better opportunities is part of the strategy, especially when market prices do not offer a sufficient margin of safety.

Temperament

We believe good investing requires emotional control, independent judgment, and the willingness to look inactive when patience is the right decision.

Transparency

Partners should understand the businesses we study, the risks we accept, and the reasons we choose to act or pass.

Alignment

We think about partner capital with the same care we expect for our own: conservatively, clearly, and over a long time horizon.

Who the family fund is built for.

Chaudhari Partners is intended for families, founders, business owners, and professionals who understand that compounding often depends on what an investor avoids as much as what an investor owns.

The right partner values thoughtful underwriting, measured communication, and a realistic discussion of risk. This is not built for short-term performance chasing, constant trading, or market commentary for its own sake.

We seek partners who value:

  • Long-term thinking
  • Discipline over activity
  • Clear communication
  • Alignment over hype
  • A realistic view of risk
  • Patience when cash is prudent
  • Business quality before price action
  • Plain-English investment reasoning
